<p><p>The Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM) detectors market is primarily categorized into analog and digital types. Analog SiPMs provide a continuous output proportional to the incoming light, making them suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity and fast response. In contrast, digital SiPMs convert the light signal into discrete digital counts, enhancing noise immunity and simplifying signal processing. Both types serve diverse applications, including medical imaging, radiation detection, and high-energy physics, catering to the growing demand for precise and reliable photon detection technologies.</p></p>
