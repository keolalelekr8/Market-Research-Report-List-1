<p><p>The Compact Camera Module (CCM) market encompasses two primary sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for professional applications. They typically consume more power and are more expensive. Conversely, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for mobile devices and consumer electronics. The choice between these technologies often hinges on application requirements and cost considerations.</p></p>
