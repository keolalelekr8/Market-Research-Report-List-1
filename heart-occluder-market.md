<p><strong>The Heart Occluder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"ASD Occluder"</li><li>"VSD Occluder"</li><li>"PDA Occluder"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heart Occluder market is segmented into three main types: ASD Occluders, VSD Occluders, and PDA Occluders. ASD Occluders are designed for treating atrial septal defects, which are holes between the heart's upper chambers. VSD Occluders target ventricular septal defects, allowing closure between the heart's lower chambers. PDA Occluders are specifically for patent ductus arteriosus, an abnormal connection between the aorta and pulmonary artery. Each type provides minimally invasive solutions to improve cardiac function and prevent complications.</p></p>
