<p><p>Natural gas pipeline markets are divided into three main types: gathering, transportation, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines collect natural gas from production sites and transport it to processing facilities. Transportation pipelines carry large volumes of processed gas over long distances to storage or distribution points. Finally, distribution pipelines deliver natural gas from transportation lines directly to consumers, including residential, commercial, and industrial users. Each pipeline type plays a crucial role in the overall supply chain of natural gas.</p></p>
