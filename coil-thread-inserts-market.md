<p><p>Coil thread inserts are utilized to repair stripped threads and enhance the strength of threaded holes in various materials. The market offers two main types: tanged thread inserts and tangless thread inserts. Tanged thread inserts, featuring small tangs, are designed for easy installation and removal, providing strong engagement with the host material. In contrast, tangless thread inserts are designed without tangs, allowing for smooth insertion and reduced risk of contamination, making them suitable for precision applications where cleanliness is crucial.</p></p>
