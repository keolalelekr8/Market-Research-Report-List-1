<p><strong>The Intravenous Membrane Oxygenator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Membrane Oxygenator</li><li>Hole Polypropylene Membrane Oxygenator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intravenous Membrane Oxygenator market consists of two primary types: Silicon Membrane Oxygenators and Hole Polypropylene Membrane Oxygenators. Silicon Membrane Oxygenators leverage a silicon-based membrane for gas exchange, offering high permeability and biocompatibility, making them suitable for extended use. In contrast, Hole Polypropylene Membrane Oxygenators utilize a microporous polypropylene structure, providing a unique filter-like function that enhances oxygen transfer while minimizing blood contact. Both types cater to critical care environments, supporting patients requiring oxygenation during medical procedures.</p></p>
