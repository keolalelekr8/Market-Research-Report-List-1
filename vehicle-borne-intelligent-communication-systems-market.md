<p><p>Vehicle-borne Intelligent Communication Systems (VICS) encompass two main market types: Closed Systems and Open Systems. Closed Systems are proprietary solutions tailored for specific vehicle models or brands, ensuring high security and optimized performance but lacking interoperability with external technologies. In contrast, Open Systems promote interoperability and adaptability across different platforms, allowing various devices and applications to communicate effectively. This flexibility fosters innovation and integration but may raise security concerns due to more accessible interfaces and connections with external networks.</p></p>
