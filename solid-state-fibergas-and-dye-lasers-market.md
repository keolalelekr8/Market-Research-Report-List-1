<p><p>Solid-state, fiber, gas, and dye lasers are different types of laser technologies, each with unique characteristics and applications. Solid-state lasers utilize a solid gain medium, typically a crystal or glass doped with ions, offering high efficiency and versatility. Fiber lasers employ optical fibers as the gain medium, known for their compact design and excellent beam quality. Gas lasers, utilizing gases like helium-neon or carbon dioxide, are commonly used in cutting and engraving due to their high output power. Dye lasers use organic dyes as a lasing medium, allowing for tunable wavelengths, making them suitable for various scientific applications.</p><p>The Solid-State, Fiber, Gas, and Dye Lasers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period.
