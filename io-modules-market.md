<p><p>IO modules are essential components in automation systems, categorized primarily into three types: analog modules, digital modules, and other modules. Analog modules handle continuous signals, translating varying inputs like temperature and pressure into readable data. Digital modules work with discrete signals, managing binary data for operations such as on/off controls. Other modules encompass specialized functions, including communication interfaces and conversion units. Together, these modules enable seamless data acquisition, control, and communication in industrial applications.</p></p>
