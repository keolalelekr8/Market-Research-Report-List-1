<p><p>The Portable Base Station (PBS) market encompasses VHF (Very High Frequency) and UHF (Ultra High Frequency) segments, each serving distinct communication needs. VHF, ranging from 30 to 300 MHz, is ideal for long-range communication, often used in maritime and aviation sectors. UHF, spanning 300 MHz to 3 GHz, offers better penetration through obstacles and is commonly utilized in urban environments for public safety, commercial communication, and personal use. Both VHF and UHF PBS systems enhance mobile connectivity and operational efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
