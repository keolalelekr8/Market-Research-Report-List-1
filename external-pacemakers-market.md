<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external pacemaker market is primarily categorized into single chamber and dual chamber pacemakers. A single chamber pacemaker stimulates either the right atrium or the right ventricle, making it suitable for patients with specific heart rhythm disorders. In contrast, a dual chamber pacemaker regulates both chambers, enhancing coordination between atrial and ventricular contractions, which is beneficial for patients requiring comprehensive heart rhythm management. Both types cater to varying patient needs, driven by technological advancements and clinical applications in cardiac care.</p></p>
