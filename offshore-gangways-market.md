<p><p>The offshore gangways market comprises two primary types: fixed and telescopic gangways. Fixed gangways are rigid structures that provide steady access between offshore platforms and vessels, ideal for stable environments. In contrast, telescopic gangways are adjustable, allowing for varied lengths and angles, accommodating changing tide levels and vessel movements. Both types enhance safety and operational efficiency in offshore activities, ensuring seamless transitions for personnel and equipment between ships and platforms under diverse marine conditions.</p></p>
