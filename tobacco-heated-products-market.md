<p><p>Tobacco Heated Products (THPs) can be categorized into three market types: Intermediate Heating, Ring Heating, and Compound Heating. Intermediate Heating utilizes moderate temperatures to heat tobacco, preserving its flavor while reducing harmful substances. Ring Heating employs a circular heating mechanism for uniform temperature distribution, enhancing the smoking experience. Compound Heating combines different heating methods to optimize flavor and reduce toxins, providing users with a balanced alternative. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and health considerations within the THP market.</p></p>
