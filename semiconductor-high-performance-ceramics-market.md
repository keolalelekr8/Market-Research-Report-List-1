Semiconductor Aluminum Nitride (AlN) ceramics offer excellent thermal conductivity and high melting points, making them ideal for heat dissipation applications. Semiconductor Silicon Carbide (SiC) ceramics are known for their superior hardness and thermal resistance, suitable for high-power devices. Lastly, Semiconductor Silicon Nitride (Si3N4) ceramics are recognized for their strength and oxidation resistance, enhancing reliability in electronic applications.</p></p>
