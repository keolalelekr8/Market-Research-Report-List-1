<p><p>The non-pleated air filters market is segmented based on Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV) ratings. MERV 3 and below filters primarily capture large particles, offering basic filtration suitable for household use. MERV 4 to 7 filters provide improved efficiency, capturing smaller particles and allergens, commonly used in residential and light commercial settings. MERV 8 and above filters offer high-level filtration, effectively trapping fine particles and microorganisms, making them ideal for healthcare facilities and industrial applications where air quality is critical.</p></p>
