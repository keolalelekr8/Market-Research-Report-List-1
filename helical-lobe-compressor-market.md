<p><p>The helical-lobe compressor market is segmented into three main types: oil-injected, oil-free, and liquid injection compressors. </p><p>Oil-injected compressors utilize oil for lubrication and cooling, enhancing efficiency and air quality, making them suitable for applications requiring compressed air with minimal moisture. Oil-free compressors operate without lubrication, delivering clean air, ideal for sensitive industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing. Liquid injection compressors use a liquid as a sealant, providing effective cooling and lubrication, making them versatile for various industrial applications while ensuring efficient operation.</p></p>
