<p><p>The car lead-acid battery market comprises three main types: VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries, flooded batteries, and other variants. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, making them ideal for modern vehicles. Flooded batteries, which are traditional designs, require regular maintenance and electrolyte checks but offer robustness and are typically cheaper. Other market types may include advanced lead-acid options like AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) or gel batteries, each catering to specific automotive needs, such as performance or durability.</p></p>
