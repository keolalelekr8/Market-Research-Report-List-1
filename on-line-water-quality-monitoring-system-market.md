<p><p>The On-Line Water Quality Monitoring System market comprises various methodologies, including the Electrode Method and Spectrophotometry. The Electrode Method utilizes specific electrodes to measure electrochemical properties like pH, conductivity, or ion concentration, providing real-time data on water quality. In contrast, Spectrophotometry measures light absorption or transmission through a water sample to identify and quantify contaminants. Both techniques are essential for ensuring effective monitoring of water quality in various environments, supporting regulatory compliance and environmental protection efforts.</p></p>
