<p><strong>The Car Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car ignition coil market is primarily divided into two types: single-spark and multi-spark. Single-spark ignition coils generate one spark per engine cycle, typically suitable for standard petrol engines, ensuring efficiency and reliability. Multi-spark ignition coils, however, produce multiple sparks in a single cycle, enhancing combustion and improving engine performance, especially in high-performance and racing applications. The choice between these types influences fuel efficiency, emissions, and overall engine power, driving innovations in automotive technology.</p></p>
