<p><p>Alnico magnets are divided into three main market types: cast, sintered, and bar alnico. Cast alnico magnets are produced by melting the alloy and pouring it into molds, allowing for complex shapes and sizes. Sintered alnico magnets are created by compressing a powdered mixture and then heating it, offering higher density and magnetic performance. Bar alnico magnets are specifically shaped for applications requiring longer, narrow configurations. Each type serves distinct industrial and technological applications based on their manufacturing processes and magnetic properties.</p></p>
