<p><p>The Agricultural Grade Zinc Sulfate market is primarily divided into two types: Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate and Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate. Zinc Sulfate Monohydrate, with a lower water content, is often used for soil applications and foliar sprays, providing essential zinc for crop health. Zinc Sulfate Heptahydrate, containing more water, is typically employed in soil amendments and can dissolve easily in water, making it suitable for fertigation and irrigation systems. Both forms enhance agricultural productivity and address zinc deficiency in soils.</p></p>
